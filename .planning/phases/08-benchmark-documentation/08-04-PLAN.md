Each dict in per_cluster_stats contains: cluster_id, n_poses, mean_hybrid_score,
std_hybrid_score, ci95_lower, ci95_upper, best_pose_idx.
Written by analysis/clustering.py; consumed by csv_writer.py and metadata.py.
