## 5. Data Quality Concerns

1. **T1 ceiling at 106**: The very_long SHEET category is sparse. Even at 3× oversampling,
   T1 contributes 318/2,242 = 14.2% of Phase 2 samples. This is the dominant bottleneck.

2. **Null sources in gap-fill**: 390 entries lack source annotation. Assigned peppc (1×)
   weight. This is conservative — if they're actually ppii_enriched, they'd deserve 4×.
   Without source information, use the conservative 1× default.

3. **UNUSUAL only 90 complexes**: UNUSUAL very_long complexes are fundamentally sparse
   in PepPC (loops, disordered regions, irregular conformations). 90 is close to the
   hard ceiling for this category in PepPC after exclusions.

4. **No novel data sources**: All training data remains within PepPC/PepPC-F. If v6 still
   fails on very_long complexes after training, the next step is to source data from
   RCSB PDB directly (PDB IDs from recent structures, peptide complexes ≥20aa).
